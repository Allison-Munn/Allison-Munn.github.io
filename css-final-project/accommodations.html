What Wahington DC Has To Offer

Washington DC has approximately 132 hotels in the city with 31,672 rooms. Within 1 mile of the Washington Convention Center there are 4,635 rooms.

The Hilton Crystal City Hotel is a 4-star hotel with a price of $87 dollars per night. It is a modern airport hoetl with meeting space, multiple dining options and a free shuttle.

The Hyatt Centric Arlington is another 4-star hotel with a price of $90 per night. It is a modern hotel offering refined rooms with free Wi-Fi, and an american eatery, a lobby bar, and a gym.

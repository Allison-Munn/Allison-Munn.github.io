Directions

Washington DC is located in the United States. Its located in the Mid-Atlantic region between the states of Maryland and Virgina. The Capital is approximatly 40 miles south of Baltimore, 30 miles west of Annapolis and the Chesapeake Bay, and 108 miles north of Richmond. Washington DC isn't part of a federal district, so it's not really in any state.

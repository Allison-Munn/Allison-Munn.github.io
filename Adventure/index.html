First stop, Venus

October 1, 2019

We first back-tracked to Venus and our tour guide told us that Venus was named after the Roman goddess of love. After that we made our way past Earth, Mars, and Jupiter. As we were passing by Jupiter our tour guide stopped and told us that Jupiter was the biggest planet in our solar system. By the time we were done with Jupiter we all stopped and slept for the night on our space craft. I also had to ask my mom to refill my oxygen tank for me since I had trouble taking my helmet off.
